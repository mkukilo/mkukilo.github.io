Destinations

We usually offer a very wide variety of destinatinos for you to choose from, this year we had to limit the selection a little, to accomodate for the travel limitations and so we could take better safety precautions, so you can enjoy your trip safely!

Bangkok

Bangkok, known for its street food vendors, isn't just a streetlong restaurant, but rather an amazing city. Visit the Temple of the Emerald Buddha, a sacre schrine with a reclining Buddha statue.

Seoul

Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace, a historic place with tours and a museum. Seoul is the perfect place for you if you like any kidn of pop culture.

Paris

Visit the Eiffel Tower and visit countless museums, Paris is a city like no other. The climate makes it perfect for a summer getaway and the romance is real.

San Francisco

See the Golden Gate Bridge in person. Experience the diverse array of cuisines in the cultural melting pot that is San Francisco.

Great Britain

Visit one of many hallmarks of Britain's culture. Admire the London Eye and Big Ben, visit Hyde Park and go to countless museums. Just one of many things that one city in England has to offer.

Hawaii

Visit one of many volcanoes and experience the rich culture of Hawaii. A tried and true traveler spot, Hawaii is known for its outstanding beauty. Enjoy the resorts, or visit the beaches. It's all up to you!

Jakarta

Take in the heady sights and smells of Glodok, Jakarta's historic Chinatown area, before heading towards central Jakarta to marvel at MONAS, Indonesia's National Monument to its freedom struggle.

Japan

Experience Tokyo's world famous neon glow and revel in Japan's rich culture. Afterwards, you can go to Mt. Fuji and admire the stunning landscape. Visit many museums and experience the vast quisine that Japan has to offer.

Greece

A land known for its outstanding architecture and landmarks. Greece has a lot more to offer than what meets the eye. Visit the Parthenon and the Acropolis of Athens before enjoing the cuisine of the locals.

Italy

Known for its cuisine, Italy also has a lot to offer other than cuisine. Visit one of many outstanding architectural wonders of the middle ages, before enjoing the summer touring around the island of Sicilly.

Australia

A rather unassuming spot for tourists, beloved nonetheless, Australia is a land of wonder that has a lot to offer. Visit the Great Barrier Reef and admire the amazing architecture of the Sydney Opera House before visiting Uluru.

South Africa

Visit South Africa and experience diverse cultures in Cape Town. Go to Kruger National Park, an immense game reserve that is sure to astound you.
